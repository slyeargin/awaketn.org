It's time to wake up.

Of the more than 6 million people who call Tennessee home, 52 percent are female and 23 percent are children under the age of 18—but they're often underrepresented or not represented at all in today's political and policy decisions. Women and children in Tennessee need a voice.

Our ultimate goal is to ensure that the interests of Tennessee’s women and children are addressed in both pending and existing legislation through public education campaigns, lobbying, supporting candidates who advocate for our initiatives, and voter mobilization.

Advocates for Women's And Kids' Equality (AWAKE) is a progressive, issues-based organization that advocates for public policies to improve the wellness, safety, opportunity, and equality for women and children in Tennessee.
